Connection Websites LLC (“Connection Websites”, “we”, “us”, or “our”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect on connectionwebsites.com (the “Site”), how we use it, and the choices you have.

1) Who We Are & Contact

Data Controller:Connection Websites LLC
Email:contact@connectionwebsites.com
Location:Orlando, FL, United States

2) Information We Collect

  • Information you provide: details sent via our contact methods or emails.
  • Usage & device data: pages viewed, approximate location, device/browser, referrer collected only after you grant analytics consent.
  • Cookies & similar tech: for analytics (see “Cookies & Your Choices”).

3) How We Use Information

  • Operate, maintain, and improve the Site.
  • Understand performance and content effectiveness (analytics after consent).
  • Respond to inquiries and provide support.
  • Maintain security, prevent fraud, and comply with law.

4) Analytics & Google Consent Mode

We use Google Analytics 4 with Consent Mode. By default, all storage is denied until you click “Accept” on the cookie banner. When accepted, we initialize GA with IP anonymization. If you decline, Analytics won’t set/read analytics cookies.

  • Tool: Google Analytics 4 (Google LLC)
  • Data: device, browser, region/city-level location, pages/events, session info.
  • Controls: Use the banner or the button.

5) Legal Bases (EEA/UK)

  • Consent for analytics cookies/measurement.
  • Legitimate interests to secure and operate the Site.
  • Contract to respond to requests or provide services.
  • Legal obligation where applicable.

6) Sharing & Transfers

  • Service providers (e.g., Google for analytics, subject to your consent).
  • Business transfers (e.g., merger, acquisition).
  • Legal compliance (e.g., lawful requests).

We are US-based. International transfers use appropriate safeguards where required.

7) Retention

We keep personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes described or as required by law. Analytics retention is managed inside Google Analytics and kept no longer than needed for trend analysis.

8) Your Rights & Choices

  • Cookies & analytics: change your choice anytime using the banner or buttons on this page.
  • Access / correction / deletion: request these where applicable.
  • EEA/UK: rights to restrict/object and to data portability; complain to your authority.
  • California (CPRA): request to know, delete, or correct; opt out of “sharing” for cross-context ads. We do not sell personal information.

To exercise rights, email contact@connectionwebsites.com. We may verify your identity.

9) Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards, but no method is 100% secure.

10) Children’s Privacy

The Site is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect their personal information.

11) Changes to This Policy

We may update this Policy. When we do, we’ll update the “Last updated” date at the top of this page.

12) How to Contact Us

Questions or requests: contact@connectionwebsites.com
